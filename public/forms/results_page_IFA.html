LifVest recommends a personal financial advisor

We believe you are suited for a Morgan Stanley financial advisor to manage your investments. With a financial advisor, your investments are a constant conversation between you and your wealth manager. Since you do not have the time or desire to manage your money yourself, your private wealth manager will follow markets and invest for you. One of the most valuable aspects of using a personal financial advisor is they answer your questions about investing, while personal brokerage accounts and robo-advisors do not.

What does Morgan Stanley charge?

Wealth managers make money by charging a management fee, a percentage of your money. In addition, the brokerage account you utilize charges commissions for transactions. The costs for personal advisors is variable. Broadly speaking, personal financial advisors are more costly than other options for investing, but they provide personal service and human expertise.
